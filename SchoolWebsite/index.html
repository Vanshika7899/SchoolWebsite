Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan

Kendriya Vidyalaya started its first academic session on 1st April 1964 and in a very short span of time, the school has carved a niche for itself as one of the eminent CBSE Schools of delhi. The School has created a benchmark in the field of senior secondary education by adopting the latest technology and tools to impart education through unique smart class system. The school is child-focused, aesthetically designed and adaptive with a view to promote learning, support collaboration and provide a personalized and stimulating environment to all.

standards

Primary: from I to V

At Kendriya Vidyalaya, we understand that the early years of a child's education are crucial in shaping their future. Our primary school program goes beyond academic instruction, focusing on the holistic development of each child. We foster creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, ensuring that every child’s intellectual curiosity is sparked and nurtured. We also emphasize the importance of social and emotional growth, as we believe these aspects are as vital as academic success. Through collaborative activities, group discussions, and interactive learning experiences, children learn how to work together, respect diverse opinions, and develop empathy and confidence. Our teachers, who are not just skilled educators but passionate mentors, guide students through this journey with patience and care, ensuring each child feels valued and supported. With a focus on building strong values and character, Kendriya Vidyalaya strives to create well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient but also responsible, compassionate, and ready to face the challenges of the future with confidence.

secondary: from VI to X

At Kendriya Vidyalaya, we recognize that the middle school years (Grades 6 to 10) are a pivotal time in a child's educational development. During this phase, students begin to transition from the foundational learning of the primary years to more complex and abstract concepts. Our middle school program is designed to support this transition by promoting a deeper understanding of subjects, fostering academic independence, and encouraging intellectual curiosity. We aim to provide a balanced curriculum that challenges students while also supporting their emotional and social growth. In addition to core subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Language Arts, our students are encouraged to explore a wide range of electives such as art, music, physical education, and computer science. This approach ensures that students not only build strong academic skills but also develop their creative, physical, and technological abilities. Critical thinking is a central focus of our teaching philosophy. Students are encouraged to ask questions, analyze information, and think logically and critically about the world around them. We provide opportunities for collaborative learning, group projects, and hands-on experiences, allowing students to work together, share ideas, and develop essential problem-solving skills.

Senior Secondary: from XI to X

At Kendriya Vidyalaya, we understand that Class 11th and 12th are critical years for shaping a student's academic and professional future. Our senior secondary program is designed to providen a strong foundation through academic excellence, critical thinking and practical learning. We offer a broad range of subject across Science, Commerce and Humanity streams, focusing on conceptual clarity and real-world application. Our experienced teachers act as mentors, providing personalized guidance to help student achieve their goals. Through interactive lessons, project-based learning and regular assessments, we ensure students are well-prepared for competitive exams and higher education. Beyond academics, we emphasize holistic development by offering extracurricular activities, career counseling and skill-building programs. Students are encouraged to participate in sports, cultural events and leadership opportunities, fostering their confidence and all-rounder growth. At Kendriya Vidyalaya, we aim to nurture responsible, disciplined and resilient individulas equipped to excel in their chosen paths and contribute meaningfully to society. Our commitment is to help every student unlock their potential and achieve lifelong success.

Admission

Kendriya Vidyalaya follows the CBSE syllabus, within which flexibility of methodology and the use of experimental learning systems is encouraged. This approach helps to eliminate the need of learning by memorization and develops a healthy and positive attitude towards learning. The system of evaluation takes into account the individual nature of every child and tries to give an adequate feedback on the student’s learning, without putting undue pressure on him/her. As the child progresses through the classes, the assessment is gradually modified to prepare him/her for external tests and competitive exams.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ADMISSION For I : Age should be 6+ years as on 31st March of the year of admission.
